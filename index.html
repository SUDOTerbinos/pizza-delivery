About Us

Our story...

Welcome to Pizza Heaven, where every slice tells a story of passion, tradition, and the joy of sharing a meal with loved ones. From Humble Beginnings Our journey began in a small, rustic kitchen in the heart of Sheger city. Founded by Terbinos Abebe, a passionate chef with a dream, Pizza Heaven started as a local favorite, known for its authentic recipes and warm, inviting atmosphere. Terbinos Abebe spent years perfecting the art of pizza making, traveling across Italy to learn from the masters and bring back the secrets of true Italian pizza. The Magic of Fresh Ingredients At Pizza Heaven, we believe that the best pizzas start with the best ingredients. Every morning, our team selects the freshest vegetables, the finest cheeses, and the highest quality meats to ensure that every bite is a burst of flavor. Our dough is handmade daily, allowed to rise slowly for a perfectly crisp yet chewy crust, and our sauces are simmered to perfection with a blend of secret spices. A Community Favorite Over the years, Pizza Heaven has grown from a small pizzeria into a beloved community hub. We've hosted countless birthday parties, family gatherings, and celebrations, becoming a cherished part of our customers' lives. Our walls are adorned with photos and memories of happy moments shared over our pizzas, and our team is more like a family, always ready to welcome you with a smile. Innovation Meets Tradition While we cherish our roots and traditional recipes, we also love to innovate. Our menu features a delightful mix of classic favorites and creative new flavors, inspired by global culinary trends. From our signature Margherita to our bold BBQ Chicken Pizza, there's something for every palate. We also offer a range of gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan options, because we believe everyone should enjoy the magic of pizza. Our Commitment to You Pizza Heaven isn't just about food; it's about creating experiences. We're committed to providing exceptional service, fast and reliable delivery, and a menu that's always evolving to delight our customers. Our mission is to make every meal a memorable one, whether you're dining in with us or enjoying a pizza at home. Looking Ahead As we look to the future, we're excited to continue our journey with you. We're constantly exploring new flavors, expanding our delivery areas, and finding ways to give back to our community. Thank you for being a part of our story. We can't wait to share many more delicious moments with you.

"Best pizza ever!" - Terbinos Abebe
